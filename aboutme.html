About me

Hello, my name is Siti NurulSyafika Binti Shafie and I will be 20 this year. I was born in Terengganu State on May 24, 2003. The place where I live is in Dungun, Terengganu. In a family of ten siblings, I am the youngest. I have four sisters and five brothers.


I am a semester 4 diploma student in the faculty of information management at the University of Technology Mara (UiTM) Sungai Petani Campus in Kedah. This is my final year of the diploma programme. I'm hoping to finish my diploma with good grades this semester. For the first session, I aim to continue my degree in the library course.


The goal of my finishing my degree is to fulfil my parents' expectations, and I also want to study further so that my knowledge of the library is more thorough. In reality, I considered the criteria required while moving to the workforce, which prefers students to have a degree rather than a diploma. However, this is simply my perspective, and I elected to continue with the degree because I wanted to.


Besides that, I have an expensive ambition: I want to travel abroad with my friends, and my dream is to attend a concert by a singer group that I really adore one day. In terms of hobbies, I enjoy kdramas and kpop.
